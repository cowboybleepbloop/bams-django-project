Checking Accounts

Thought all checking accounts were more or less the same? Think again. Look for accounts that don't charge you fees to access your money and can earn you money over time with an APY.

Savings Accounts

Savings accounts are federally insured financial products that can help your money grow. The best savings accounts have high annual percentage yields, or APYs. The higher the APY, the more money you'll earn over time.

Why choosing the right bank and account type matters

The main difference between an online savings account and checking account is how you plan to use the money. A checking account is typically used for everyday transactions and easier access to your money, while a savings account is basically an investment account that earns interest on a balance that grows over time. A savings account is better for building up emergency funds and working toward larger financial goals.
